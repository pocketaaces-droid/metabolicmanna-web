Who Should Be Careful with Fasting

Certain individuals should consult a doctor before attempting any fasting protocol. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • People with diabetes or blood sugar regulation issues
  • Individuals with a history of eating disorders
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • Children and adolescents under 18
  • Anyone taking prescription medications
  • People with a history of heart disease, kidney disease, or liver disease
  • Those who are underweight or malnourished
